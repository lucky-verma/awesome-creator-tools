## Video Creation & Editing

*Tools for recording, editing, and producing video content.*

### Desktop Editors

- [DaVinci Resolve](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve/) - Professional editing, color grading, and effects. Free version is genuinely powerful.
- [Kdenlive](https://kdenlive.org/) - Open-source editor with multi-track timeline and broad format support.
- [OpenShot](https://www.openshot.org/) - Simple open-source editor, good for beginners.
- [Shotcut](https://shotcut.org/) - Cross-platform editor with native timeline editing.

### Programmatic Video

*For automated workflows and batch processing.*

- [Auto-Editor](https://github.com/WyattBlue/auto-editor) - Automatically removes silence from videos. Great for podcasts and talking-head content.
- [Editly](https://github.com/mifi/editly) - Create videos programmatically via JSON. Useful for templated content.
- [FFmpeg](https://ffmpeg.org/) - The backbone of video processing. Handles encoding, transcoding, and streaming.
- [MoviePy](https://github.com/Zulko/moviepy) - Python library for video editing and compositing.

### Browser-Based

- [Clipchamp](https://clipchamp.com/) - Microsoft's browser editor with stock assets and templates.
- [Kapwing](https://www.kapwing.com/) - Collaborative online editor with AI features.

## Thumbnail & Graphics

*Design tools for thumbnails, channel art, and social graphics.*

- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) - Template-based design with YouTube thumbnail presets.
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) - Professional design tool, free for individuals.
- [Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) - Free Photoshop alternative that runs in browser. No account needed.
- [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org/) - Open-source image editor with professional features.
- [Remove.bg](https://www.remove.bg/) - AI background removal for thumbnails.

### Generators

- [ThumbsUp](https://github.com/nicholasdly/thumbs-up) - Generate thumbnails with AI.
- [Video Thumbnail Generator](https://github.com/nicholasdly/video-thumbnail-generator) - Extract frames from videos as thumbnails.

## Audio & Podcasting

*Recording, editing, hosting, and distribution for podcasters.*

### Recording & Editing

- [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) - Open-source audio editor. Been around forever, still works great.
- [Descript](https://www.descript.com/) - Edit audio by editing text. Includes transcription and overdub.
- [Riverside](https://riverside.fm/) - High-quality remote recording with separate tracks per guest.
- [Zencastr](https://zencastr.com/) - Remote podcast recording with free tier.

### Hosting & Distribution

- [Anchor](https://anchor.fm/) - Free hosting and distribution by Spotify.
- [Audiobookshelf](https://github.com/advplyr/audiobookshelf) - Self-host your podcast and audiobook library.
- [Podcastfy](https://github.com/souzatharsis/podcastfy) - Generate podcasts from text using AI.

### Enhancement

- [Adobe Podcast](https://podcast.adobe.com/) - AI-powered noise removal and enhancement.
- [Auphonic](https://auphonic.com/) - Automatic leveling and loudness normalization.

## Live Streaming

*Software and tools for Twitch, YouTube Live, and other platforms.*

### Streaming Software

- [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/) - The standard for streaming. Open-source, extensible, free.
- [Streamlabs](https://streamlabs.com/) - OBS fork with built-in alerts, overlays, and themes.
- [VDO.Ninja](https://vdo.ninja/) - Bring remote guests into your stream without downloads.

### Bots & Automation

- [Nightbot](https://nightbot.tv/) - Cloud chat bot for moderation and commands.
- [PhantomBot](https://github.com/PhantomBot/PhantomBot) - Self-hosted Twitch bot with extensive customization.
- [StreamElements](https://streamelements.com/) - All-in-one streaming toolkit with bot, overlays, and alerts.

## Social Media Management

*Schedule, publish, and manage content across platforms.*

### Scheduling

- [Buffer](https://buffer.com/) - Simple scheduling with analytics.
- [Later](https://later.com/) - Visual planner, strong on Instagram.
- [Postiz](https://github.com/gitroomhq/postiz-app) - Open-source alternative to Buffer.

### Cross-Posting

- [Repurpose.io](https://repurpose.io/) - Automatically publish to multiple platforms from one upload.
- [TikToka Studio Uploader](https://github.com/nicholasdly/tiktoka-studio-uploader) - Bulk upload to TikTok.
